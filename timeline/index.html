Introdution Evaluation Timeline Submission Organizers

Time Period and Key Deliverables

The following schedule is formulated in accordance with Greenwich Mean Time (UTC+0)

Dec 1, 2025 - Dec 6, 2025 Registration
Challenge registration opens.
Dec 7, 2025 - Jan 1, 2026 Algorithm Development
Dataset and initial code release, providing a small amount of validation data for submitting tests.
Jan 2, 2026 - Jan 15, 2026 Validation
The validating phase begins.
Jan 16, 2026 - Jan 22, 2026 Test
The test phase begins.
Jan 23, 2026 - Mar 1, 2026 Publish the final results
The challenge organizer ranks the submitted algorithms according to the prescribed evaluation method
Mar 2, 2026 - Mar 15, 2026 Submit the final report
The participating teams who have completed the submission will be required to submit a report on the algorithm implementation.
Apr 8, 2026 - Apr 11, 2026 Announcing the winners
ISBI conference begins, announcing the list of winners.
